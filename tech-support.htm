Common Technical Issues & Solutions

Experiencing technical difficulties? Our comprehensive guide will help you resolve common platform issues quickly and get back to learning.

Login & Access Issues

Problems signing in or accessing your courses?

  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Reset your password using "Forgot Password"
  • Try a different web browser
  • Check your internet connection
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily

Video Playback Problems

Videos not loading or playing correctly?

  • Check your internet speed and stability
  • Update your web browser to latest version
  • Try lowering video quality settings
  • Disable hardware acceleration in browser
  • Clear browser cache and restart

Course Progress Not Saving

Your progress isn't being recorded?

  • Ensure you're properly logged in
  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Complete entire sections before navigating away
  • Contact support if issue persists

Mobile App Issues

Problems with the mobile application?

  • Update to the latest app version
  • Restart the mobile application
  • Check device storage space
  • Reinstall the application if needed
  • Ensure stable internet connection
